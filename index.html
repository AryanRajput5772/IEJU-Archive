FAQs


What does "Notices" Section Contains ?
The Notices section displays Notices directly from the Jiwaji University noticeboard, filtered to show only those containing the keywords “B.E.” or “Result” It automatically fetches and displays the latest relevant notices for students.
  • To search for semester wise Notices use Roman numbers like "vi" for 6th sem, "iv" for 4th sem and accordingly
  • You can also search by date using the format: dd/mmm/yyyy — for example, 28/Apr/2025 (use first 3 letters of month, like Apr)
What does "Syllabus" Section Contains ?
The Syllabus section provides access to the Syllabus of each semester
Why are unit-wise notes not available ?
Currently, unit-wise notes are not available for every subject. As soon as we receive any, we will add to the IEJU archive. If anyone has unit-wise notes for any subject and would like to contribute, feel free to reach out to us via contact form.
How can I share my testimonial ?
You can share your testimonial by reaching out to us through the contact form available on the website. In the message section of the contact form, send your testimonial along with your name. We'd love to hear about your experience and feature your feedback in the IEJU archive
